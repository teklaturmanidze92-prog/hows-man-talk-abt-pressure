    adidas Spzl x C.P. Company

    $300

    adidas Spzl and C.P. Company team up again to celebrate a decade of adidas’s iconic Spzl line. Known for its Italian craftsmanship, urban-meets-military aesthetic and deeply rooted in football terrace culture and fashion, C.P. Company has gained a cult following in European streetwear since its founding in 1971. The brand’s iconic pieces, such as its Mille Miglia ‘goggle jacket,” have become symbolic of both functional and fashionable clothing on the terraces. Similarly, adidas Spzl, curated by Gary Aspden, has celebrated adidas’s archival silhouettes, making it immensely popular among terrace goers and sneaker enthusiasts across Europe. This collaboration for the Spzl’s tenth anniversary is a tribute to the connection between these two iconic brands.
